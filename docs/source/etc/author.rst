================
About the Author
================

The ROS Gazebo Gym framework is developed and maintained by the `Cognitive Robotics department`_
of the `TU Delft`_. It was created out of a Master Thesis of `Rick Staa`_ under the supervision of
`dr. Wei Pan`_.
